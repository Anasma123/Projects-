About Us

Founded in 2010, CH Academy For Education stands as a beacon of transformative education, dedicated to nurturing young minds from classes 5 to 10 with a holistic and innovative approach in English and Malayalam mediums.

Our academy combines traditional values with modern teaching methodologies to create an environment where students thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.
